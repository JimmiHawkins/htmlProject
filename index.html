Who We Are (Briefly)

Yogan is an all new vegan yoghurt company based in Plymouth. We offer a wide range of yoghurt flavours available to purchase right here, as well as supplying catering services for weddings and other events. We are huge advocates of the vegan lifestyle, and our main intentions are to make the world a better place to live

The Vegan lifestyle

Here at Yogan, we believe that all animals should be treated with respect. The needless killing and suffering of farm animals has gone on long enough, and we are doing everything in our power to stop the mistreatment of farm animals. Check out this video from the Animal Equality Youtube channel
